Here are 8 lifestyle changes you can make to lower your blood pressure and keep it down.

1. Lose extra pounds and watch your waistline. Blood pressure often increases as weight increases.

2. Exercise regularly.

3. Eat a healthy diet.

4. Reduce sodium in your diet.

5. Limit the amount of alcohol you drink.

6. Increase your activity level and exercise more

7. Reduce excess stress

8. Try meditation or yoga
